Juntos - a step towards better future.

Homelessness is a mark of failure for communities in providing basic security. Based on national reports, it’s estimated that no less than 150 million people, or about 2 percent of the world’s population, are homeless. However, about 1.6 billion, more than 20 percent of the world’s population, may lack adequate housing.. There is a need to address to this serious problem, this website provides a platform to enlist all the organizations working for this cause.

What we offer?

To Users

A platform to know about all the organizations working for this cause, there are times wherein people are not able to know about the works of many such organizations. This site functions as an aggregator of all such sites.

To Organizations

  • A platform to make a short web page about their organization
  • A platform to inform about their news / events
  • A dashboard to monitor all the contact / volunteer etc. applications and thus expand.

    • Sign Up Now.

    If you are an NGO working towards eradicating homelessness, register on our website following a few easy steps and get a link to your own page designed automatically for you which you can share for promotions / information etc..

    Our Mission.

    Several organizations are working toward eradicating homelessness and getting people off the streets. We aim at providing a solution to list such organizations, spreading awareness among the general public about the work these organizations are doing for a better community and make it accessible for citizens to contribute in terms of donations and ideas.
